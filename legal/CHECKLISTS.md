# Legal Pre‑Publish Checklists

Copyright & Plagiarism
- [ ] No verbatim copying beyond short quotations (≤75 words per source, ≤300 cumulative) with quotation marks + citation
- [ ] Paraphrasing is substantial and original; facts restated with plain language
- [ ] Citations present for all stats/claims (2–4 reputable sources)

Images & Media
- [ ] Source/license recorded (stock CC0/CC‑BY/paid) OR generative prompt saved
- [ ] No brands/logos/trademarks in images; no identifiable private individuals without consent
- [ ] Alt text meaningful; thumbnails uncluttered

Trademarks & Logos
- [ ] Nominative fair use only (referential use); no logos; no sponsorship implication
- [ ] Brand names accurate; use ®/™ only when strictly necessary (avoid)

Claims & Safety
- [ ] No medical/health/legal advice; include standard disclaimers where relevant
- [ ] Safety guidance conservative; complies with known standards (e.g., food temps)
- [ ] No defamatory statements; stick to verifiable facts

Endorsements & Affiliates
- [ ] Disclosures are clear and conspicuous near the recommendation (if applicable)
- [ ] No undisclosed comps or paid placements

Privacy & Children
- [ ] No collection of personal data in content; no minors’ personal info
- [ ] Avoid child‑directed positioning unless COPPA reviewed
