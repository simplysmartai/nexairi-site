# Image & Media Policy

Allowed
- Licensed stock (CC0/CC‑BY/paid) with recorded source and terms
- Generative images using prompts that exclude brands/logos/likenesses
- Original photography you own

Prohibited
- Brand logos, UI screenshots with trademarks, product box art
- Identifiable private people without written consent
- Hotlinking images you don’t control

Prompts (use as a suffix)
- "no brands, no logos, no text, editorial style, natural light"

Attribution
- CC‑BY: include creator + link in article citations or credits block.

Alt Text
- Describe function/context (why the image is here), not camera details.
