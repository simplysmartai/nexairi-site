# Nexairi Voice & Tone

Principles
- Human taste, automated pace: clear judgment + operational calm.
- Friendly, direct, and useful. Remove fluff; keep warmth.
- Confident but humble; invite verification with links.

Do
- Lead with outcomes, back with specifics.
- Use examples, time windows, and numbers readers can act on.
- Name trade‑offs and constraints.

Don’t
- Over‑promise or hype. No vague claims.
- Jargon without explanation. No adversarial snark.

Audience notes
- Lifestyle: practical rituals, calm hosting, realistic budgets.
- Travel: predictability, buffers, safety, timing.
- Technology: brand‑agnostic; privacy and reliability first.
- Sports: stakes + storylines; respectful to casual fans.
