Use Airtable or Supabase with merchant, SKU, price, tags, affiliate link, compliance notes, shipping cutoff, and region availability. Sol checks disclosures before delivery.

Deployment checklist

  • Swap webhook URLs and test in staging.
  • Enable validation + spam controls (hCaptcha, Turnstile, or rate limiting).
  • Update `/tools/gift-finder-portal.html` with live brand colors or embed inside your CMS.
  • Document privacy/consent language in the form footer if collecting personal data.
  • Send the first briefs through Atlas + Vega + Nova + Echo and review outputs with Sol.
