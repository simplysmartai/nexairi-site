Delicious Thanksgiving Desserts

By Sarah R. · 7 min read

No-Bake Pumpkin Pie with Gingersnap Crust

Ditch the oven stress this Thanksgiving with a delightful no-bake pumpkin pie! Its creamy, spiced filling rests on a crisp gingersnap crust, offering all the classic flavors without the fuss. This beauty is perfect for making a day or two ahead, requiring just an overnight chill to set perfectly. Simply cover tightly with plastic wrap and keep refrigerated until serving for easy transport.

Bourbon Pecan Tart with Chocolate

Elevate your dessert table with this sophisticated Bourbon Pecan Tart, boasting rich chocolate undertones that perfectly complement the toasted pecans and hint of bourbon. It's a truly decadent treat that tastes even better when given time for the flavors to meld. Prepare it a day in advance and let it chill overnight; it travels beautifully when covered in a sturdy pie carrier.

Pumpkin-Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake

For a show-stopping dessert that's as delicious as it is beautiful, try this Pumpkin-Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake. The vibrant pumpkin meets rich chocolate in a creamy, dreamy confection that will impress every guest. This cheesecake absolutely thrives on being made ahead; chill it overnight (or even two days!) for the best texture and flavor. Transport it carefully in its springform pan for a picture-perfect presentation.

Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars

These Caramel Apple Cheesecake Bars offer all the comforting flavors of fall in a wonderfully portable format, making them ideal for potlucks and gatherings. With a buttery crust, creamy cheesecake layer, and sweet apple-caramel topping, they're simply irresistible. They are fantastic to make a day in advance, allowing the flavors to fully develop after an overnight chill. Store them in an airtight container in the fridge for easy transport and serving.

No-Bake Pumpkin Lush

This No-Bake Pumpkin Lush is a layered dessert dream, combining a buttery pecan crust with layers of cream cheese, pumpkin pudding, and whipped topping. It's an absolute crowd-pleaser and a fantastic choice when you need a dessert that can hold up beautifully. Prepare it a day ahead to ensure it's perfectly chilled and set, then cover it tightly and keep refrigerated until it's time to serve.

Classic Sweet Potato Pie

Embrace the comforting flavors of the South with this Classic Sweet Potato Pie, a beloved Thanksgiving staple that offers a delicious alternative to pumpkin. Its creamy, subtly spiced filling in a flaky crust is pure holiday magic. This pie is a champion for make-ahead planning; bake it a day in advance and let it cool completely before storing loosely covered at room temperature or refrigerated.

Recipe link

Easy Vegan Pumpkin Pie

Ensure everyone at your table can enjoy a slice of Thanksgiving tradition with this Easy Vegan Pumpkin Pie. Crafted with a creamy, perfectly spiced filling and a flaky crust, it proves that plant-based desserts can be just as decadent. This pie is ideal for making ahead; let it chill overnight to firm up beautifully. For travel, secure it in a pie box and keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

Gluten-Free Pecan Pie Bars

Bring the classic taste of pecan pie to your gluten-free guests with these convenient and delicious Pecan Pie Bars. They offer the perfect balance of buttery crust, gooey filling, and crunchy pecans in a shareable, easy-to-handle format. These bars are fantastic for making ahead; prepare them a day or two in advance and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. They're incredibly portable for holiday gatherings!

No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars (DF/GF Option)

For a crowd-pleasing, allergy-friendly dessert, these No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars are an absolute winner. With options for dairy-free and gluten-free variations, everyone can enjoy their rich, chocolate-peanut butter goodness. They are incredibly simple to make and set up perfectly with an overnight chill. Cut into squares and layer with parchment in an airtight container for easy, mess-free transport to any holiday event.
