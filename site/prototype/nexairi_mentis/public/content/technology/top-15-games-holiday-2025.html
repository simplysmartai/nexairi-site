Cross-Platform Greatness: The 15 Games Defining Holiday 2025

Whether you're on PS5, Xbox Series X, or Switch, these are the titles worth your time this season.

By Sami W. · 12 min read

The console wars are over; the content wars are in full swing. This holiday season offers an incredible lineup across all major platforms. We've curated the top 15 titles that deliver the best experiences, from massive RPGs to tight, competitive shooters.

The Heavy Hitters

1. Grand Theft Auto VI (Pre-Order/Hype)

Platform: PS5, Xbox Series X
The Pitch: The most anticipated game of the decade returns to Vice City. While release dates shift, securing a pre-order or special edition is the ultimate gift for the forward-thinking gamer.

2. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Platform: Nintendo Switch
The Pitch: A masterpiece of open-world design. Build vehicles, explore the skies, and dive into the depths. It remains essential playing for anyone with a Switch.

Buy Now

3. Starfield: Shattered Space

Platform: Xbox Series X, PC
The Pitch: Bethesda's space epic gets darker and deeper. The expansion adds mystery and horror elements to the vast galaxy.

Buy Now

4. Marvel's Spider-Man 2

Platform: PS5
The Pitch: Swing through New York with Venom and Kraven breathing down your neck. The definitive superhero experience.

Buy Now

5. Mario Kart World

Platform: Nintendo Switch
The Pitch: The biggest kart racer ever made. An open-hub world connects classic tracks with new, gravity-defying circuits. It’s the definitive multiplayer experience for Switch.

Buy Now

Digital consoles (PS5 Digital, Xbox Series S) cannot play physical discs. Always check which console the recipient has before buying a physical game box.
