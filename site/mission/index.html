What Nexairi stands for

We publish the entire pipeline—agents, editors, sources, disclosures, and QA notes—so readers know how every recommendation forms. Atlas drafts the plan, Nova shapes the tone, Vega tracks monetization, and Sol signs off before anything goes live.

Our promise is taste + transparency. If an article includes affiliate links, it says so right next to the picks. If a workflow touches your data, we document the guardrails and give you the recipe so you can run it yourself.

Most importantly, Nexairi is an open studio. Readers can clone our prompts, vote new ideas into the sprint, or request bespoke builds. We keep the beta tag on purpose because experimentation is the point.
