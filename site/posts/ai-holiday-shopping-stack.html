AI Holiday Shopping Stack

Prompts, extensions, and safeguards that keep gift scouting fast, private, and under budget.

Home Our Mission Lifestyle Travel Technology Sports Sandbox Contact
By Michael T. · 7 min read

TL/TD

Use one AI for scouting, one for price checks, and one for fact-checking reviews. Keep prompts tight, log your picks, and cap a weekly budget inside your notes app.

Scouting: run a single prompt that includes age, vibe, hard no’s, price ceiling, and shipping deadline. Force three outputs: hero pick, safe pick, wildcard. Save to a table with links and notes.

Price checks: use a lightweight extension that compares trusted retailers and flags refurb vs. new. Avoid random marketplaces for anything with power or skin contact. Track tax and shipping—holiday fees creep.

Review sanity: ask an AI to summarize verified-buyer reviews and extract failure modes (breaks, sizing, smell, returns). If you can’t see verified reviews, skip the product. Never let AI “invent” scores.

Privacy: run in a separate browser profile, block third-party cookies, and clear the stack weekly. If you’re sharing lists, export to PDF instead of live sheets with tracking on.

Budget: set a weekly ceiling. When you log a gift, subtract immediately. Keep a “pending” column so you don’t double-buy while you wait on shipping or stock alerts.
