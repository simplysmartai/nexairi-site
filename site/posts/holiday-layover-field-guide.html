Holiday Layover Field Guide

Travel

By Sarah R. · 6 min read

Kits: tech (charger, splitter, power bank, offline movies), comfort (eye mask, socks, light scarf), food (protein bar, nuts, electrolyte packet). Keep them in one pouch for quick bag swaps.

Loop: find a seat near daylight or a quiet corner; plug in, set a 25-minute timer to walk every hour, and hydrate early. If you have lounge access, set a “leave” alarm so you don’t miss boarding in crowded gates.

Weather: match playlists to conditions—rain = calm acoustics, snow = warm jazz, sun = light upbeat. It sounds small, but controlled audio keeps stress down when announcements spike.

Time math: board early in winter; overhead bin space disappears faster with bulky coats. If you’re tight on connections, pick a row near the front and stow under-seat so you can exit fast.
