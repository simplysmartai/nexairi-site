Matter Stack, Zero Drill

Routers, hubs, and sensors renters can plug in tonight and reclaim in under ten minutes.

Renters need portable automations: a dual-band router that won’t choke on multiple hubs, a Matter bridge that falls back to local control, and peel-and-stick sensors you can reclaim when you move.

Start with one smart plug and a motion sensor in the entry, then add a soft-mount switch in your main room. Keep a “reset” bag with original screws, adhesive tabs, and a spare Ethernet patch so teardown is under ten minutes.

Privacy note: favor devices with local APIs and clear firmware policies. If a hub can’t keep working when the internet drops, it doesn’t make the cut.
