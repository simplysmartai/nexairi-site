College Bowl Season Playbook

Sports

By Noah V. · 6 min read

TL/TD

Pick five must-watch games, one upset hunt, and one hometown bowl. Block time, prep snacks, and keep mornings free for movement and errands.

Schedule triage: circle the playoff games, your alma mater, and one “defense clinic” matchup. Everything else is optional. Set calendar holds so family/guests know what’s sacred and what can move.

Viewing stack: one main screen, one tablet for alternate angles or stats. Keep your phone on Do Not Disturb. If you host, pick one signature snack and one drink, not five—you’re running a watch, not a banquet.

Wellness: hit a 20-minute walk or mobility session before the first kickoff. Hydrate and front-load protein so you’re not mainlining chips all day. Between games, step outside for 15 minutes to reset eyes and head.

Data: follow one analyst you trust for live notes; mute the hot-take firehose. Track simple metrics—third-down efficiency, turnovers, explosive plays—so you can spot who’s actually controlling the game.

Cleanup: cap the night with 10 minutes of tidying and a short stretch. Don’t bring the TV glow to bed; tomorrow’s games (or life) will feel better if you land this one calmly.
