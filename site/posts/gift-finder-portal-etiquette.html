Gift Finder Portal Etiquette

How to brief GIF-AI, when to override it, and how to hand off results gracefully.

By Sarah R. · 6 min read
gifting automation culture

The best GIF-AI briefs read like a friend introducing someone new: age, vibe, hard no’s, budget, and occasion. Skip the life story. Three to five crisp lines beat a paragraph of adjectives.

When results land, override liberally if a pick feels off-brand for your recipient. Add one personal touch—a note, a local add-on—to avoid the “bot picked this” feeling. If you hand off to a teammate, include the short brief you used so they understand the context, not just the links.

Finally, disclose automation. A simple line like “Pulled these from our GIF-AI shortlist and added my notes” builds trust and sets expectations for future requests.
