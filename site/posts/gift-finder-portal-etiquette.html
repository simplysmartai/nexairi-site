Gift Finder Portal Etiquette

How to brief GIF-AI, when to override it, and how to hand off results gracefully.

By Sarah R. · 6 min read
gifting automation culture

The best GIF-AI briefs read like a friend introducing someone new: age, vibe, hard no’s, budget, and occasion. Skip the life story. Three to five crisp lines beat a paragraph of adjectives and keep the model focused.

When results land, override anything that feels off-brand for your recipient. Add one personal touch—a handwritten note, locally made add-on—to avoid the “bot picked this” feeling. If you hand off to a teammate, include the short brief you used so they know the context, not just the links.

Last, disclose automation. A simple line like “Pulled these from our GIF-AI shortlist and added my notes” sets expectations and builds trust for future requests. That transparency also helps you refine prompts as you see which picks resonate.
