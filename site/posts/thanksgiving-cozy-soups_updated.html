Cozy Thanksgiving Soups

By Sarah R. · 7 min read

Creamy Vegan Mushroom Soup

Dive into the rich, earthy flavors of this luxurious vegan mushroom soup, perfect for chilly evenings. Naturally dairy-free and easily gluten-free, it proves comfort food doesn’t need compromise. Make a big batch, chill, and reheat gently on the stovetop for a ready-to-serve potluck hero.

Recipe: Rainbow Plant Life

Slow-Cooker Butternut Squash Soup

Let the slow cooker do the work with this velvety squash soup—naturally vegetarian and easily vegan with vegetable broth. It’s sweet-savory comfort that travels well right in the crock. Freeze portions, then thaw and reheat gently for a no-fuss second showing.

Recipe: Love & Lemons

Historic Creamy Pumpkin Soup (from 1651)

Take a step back in time with this simple, silky pumpkin soup that still tastes modern. Vegetarian and easily dairy-free with plant milk, it’s a make-ahead gem. Cool fully, portion, and reheat slowly, whisking to bring back the velvety finish.

Recipe: Cookie + Kate

Elegant Parsnip Apple Soup with Lotus Root Chips

Sweet parsnip meets tart apple in a smooth, elegant bowl, finished with delicate lotus root chips. Vegetarian, naturally gluten-free, and easily dairy-free with plant cream. Make the base ahead, reheat gently, and fry the chips just before serving for crunch.

Recipe: Betty Liu

Warming Pumpkin Curry Soup

Pumpkin, coconut milk, and warming spices create a cozy, lightly spicy curry soup. It’s vegan, gluten-free, and reheats beautifully with a splash of broth. Pack in jars and warm on the stovetop for a fast, fragrant opener.

Recipe: Jar of Lemons

Hearty Zuppa Toscana Soup

Creamy broth, sausage, potatoes, and kale make for a rich, crowd-friendly classic. Not dairy-free, but easy to reheat over low heat; add a splash of broth if it thickens. Transport in a thermos pot and ladle to order.

Recipe: Creme De La Crumb

Comforting Tortellini Soup with Sausage & Spinach

Cheese tortellini, browned sausage, and spinach in a creamy broth—weeknight-friendly and hearty. Make the base ahead; cook tortellini just before serving or keep separate when transporting to avoid soggy pasta. Reheat gently and stir in pasta to serve.

Recipe: Budget Bytes

Flavorful Slow-Cooker Turkey Chili

Lean ground turkey, beans, and spices simmer low and slow for a hearty, protein-rich chili. It’s dairy-free and gluten-free; pack toppings separately. Reheat gently and add a splash of broth if it thickens in the fridge.

Recipe: Ambitious Kitchen

Wholesome Slow-Cooker Ham and Bean Soup

Smoky ham hock, tender beans, and vegetables simmer into a deeply comforting bowl. Naturally gluten-free; keep it dairy-free by default. Reheat on low and add broth to keep it silky after travel.

Recipe: Art and the Kitchen

Easy Chicken and Andouille Gumbo

For a heartier bowl, this gumbo layers a nutty roux with tender chicken, spicy andouille, and classic Louisiana aromatics. It’s naturally dairy-free and even better after an overnight rest when the flavors deepen. Transport it in a Dutch oven, reheat slowly, and add a splash of stock if it thickens in the fridge.

Recipe: Louisiana Cookin'
