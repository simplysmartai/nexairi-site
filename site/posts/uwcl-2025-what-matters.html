Format tweaks, press triggers, and the clubs reshaping knockout math this season.

Press lines are higher, rotations are deeper, and the knockout bracket rewards teams that manage minutes early. Track mid-block triggers and how often fullbacks invert—those patterns decide who survives the compressed schedule.

Watch the clubs that can swap shapes without burning subs. In a crowded calendar, adaptability beats pure pace. If a team can't defend transition within three passes, they'll be punished by anyone with fresher legs and an organized press.

Bookmark possession chains and shot quality, not just scorelines. The teams that keep clean build sequences under fatigue are the ones that last in May. Look for sides whose xG doesn't collapse after the 70th minute—that's usually the stamina tell.
