Layover Playlists by Weather

Pair audio moods with rain, snow, or sun so airports feel intentional.

air travel planning

Set three playlists: rain (slow, warm), snow (cozy instrumentals), sun (light, upbeat). Pull them up as soon as you hit the terminal so you control the soundscape instead of the PA system.

Match each playlist with a tiny ritual: rain gets tea and a corner seat, snow gets a blanket scarf and a book, sun gets a short walk and water. This keeps layovers feeling intentional rather than wasted time.

Save them offline, keep headphones charged, and set a 10-minute timer before boarding to reset your brain for the next leg.
