Carry-On Rituals for Red-Eye Routes

Packing cues, compression hacks, and sleep tricks that make the overnight flight feel less like a dare.

By Noah V. · 5 min read
packing air travel wellness

Build a repeatable overnight kit: compression socks, soft neck wrap, and a mini sleep mask. Keep it in a clear pouch so you can grab it without re-packing your whole bag.

Eat light, hydrate early, and set a 90-minute timer after takeoff to dim screens and swap into your sleep kit. Save one upbeat playlist for landing so your brain knows the flight is over.

On arrival, move: 10 minutes of walking, water, and daylight. You’re not trying to beat jet lag—just trimming the worst of it so you can enjoy the first day.
